About Nike

Nike was started in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight. They make amazing products like shoes and clothes for sports. Nike has innovations like Nike Air for cushioning and focuses on sustainability. They work with stars like Michael Jordan and Serena Williams.
